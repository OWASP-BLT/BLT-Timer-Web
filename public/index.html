Everything a Bug Hunter Needs

Purpose-built features that fit naturally into your security research workflow — from the first recon scan to the final report.

Time Tracking

Start and stop sessions with a single click. BLT automatically accumulates daily, weekly, and per-project totals so you always know where your hours go. Detailed breakdowns help you spot productive patterns and eliminate wasted time.

GitHub Integration

Link sessions directly to GitHub issues and pull requests. Time entries are annotated with commit hashes, repo names, and issue numbers so your logs double as an audit trail. Works seamlessly with OWASP BLT issues out of the box.

Privacy First

No telemetry, no behavioral analytics, no selling of your data. Every time entry belongs to you — export it at any time in JSON or CSV format. BLT is open source, fully auditable, and hosted under the OWASP umbrella.

BACON Rewards

Earn BACON points for every logged session, reported bug, and day-streak maintained. Climb the leaderboard, unlock badges, and redeem points within the OWASP BLT ecosystem. Staying productive has never tasted so good.

Up and Running in Minutes

Three simple steps from sign-up to your first tracked session.

Step 1

Start a Session

Sign in with your OWASP BLT account, pick a project or bug program, and hit Start. BLT begins recording the moment you click.

Step 2

Track Activity

Log notes, tag activities (recon, exploit, report), and link to GitHub issues as you work. Everything is timestamped and searchable from your dashboard.

Step 3

Earn Rewards

Stop your session when done. BACON points are awarded automatically based on hours logged and bugs reported. Watch your rank rise on the global leaderboard.
